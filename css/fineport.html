A collection of my work in mixed media, acrylic, and photography.

Drawing/Painting/Photography

Below is my work in photography, painting, and drawing. Most of this work was created during my time in AP Design and Composition, a class I took during during high school. Taken in many locations, mostly in Florida and Georgia, my photography is brightly colored and vibrant. It expresses the beauty of natural and coastal locations. My painting consists of acrylic paints over original photographs and also features backgrounds with collage elements.
